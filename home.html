About Us

The History of our Hotel

Nestled along the sun-kissed shores of Sri Lanka, Whispering Waves Retreat invites you to a coastal sanctuary steeped in history and embraced by modern elegance. Originally a humble fishing village in the early 20th century, this beachside haven has evolved into a cherished family legacy, seamlessly blending its colonial past with contemporary comfort. Explore the architectural splendor, cultural tapestry, and sustainable harmony that define this retreat, and let each moment be a chapter in your own coastal story. Welcome to Whispering Waves, where the ocean's whispers echo through time.
